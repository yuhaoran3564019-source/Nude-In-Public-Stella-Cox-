Nude In Public
Artistic Documentary Project

This is the central homepage of an international long-term art project, documenting and exploring female lifelong nudists around the world. Created and curated by an independent Europe-based artist, together with lifelong nudist Stella Cox, who collaborates in interviews, photography, video production, and documentary storytelling.

▶ Stella Cox — Lifelong Nudist

The first woman interviewed and followed in this project. Her life, faith, philosophy, and conflict between traditional Christian background and self-chosen nudist life form the spiritual foundation of this artistic archive.
