Nude In Public

Artistic Documentary Project

About This Project

This is a long-term international art documentary project focused on female lifelong nudists and their personal stories, faith, conflicts, social struggles, and philosophical views on freedom and identity. Created by an independent Europe-based artist, in collaboration with lifelong nudist Stella Cox.

▶ Stella Cox — Lifelong Nudist

Stella is the first woman interviewed in this project. Her journey— from a strict Christian family to choosing a lifelong nudist path, surviving social conflict, homelessness, and rebirth—forms the spiritual foundation of this whole documentary universe.

Project Scope

  • Interviews with lifelong nudists (Europe / Asia / Global)
  • Documentary & Video Series
  • Artistic Photography & Realistic Storytelling
  • Faith, Identity, Conflict, Freedom
  • Behind-the-scenes of the film "Don't Laugh at Her!"

Vision

This site is an evolving art archive. More women, more interviews, more documentary footage, more philosophy, and more real lives will be added over time.
